Frequently Asked Questions

What level of algebra is covered?

Mathpop algebra covers middle school (one and two-step equations, inequalities) through college algebra (logarithms, function composition, polynomial evaluation, and geometric sequences).

Are algebra answers always integers?

Most algebra problems are designed with integer solutions for clean practice. Negative exponent problems may produce decimals, which are entered and checked with a tolerance.

Is algebra practice free?

Algebra is a premium category available with the Mathpop Premium Pass ($4.99/month or $49.99/year).

Can I practice just one algebra topic?

Yes — simply select only the topics you want (e.g., just Logarithms or just Quadratics) and Mathpop will generate only those question types for your session.

How does function composition work in Mathpop?

You'll be given two linear functions f(x) and g(x) and asked to evaluate f(g(x)) at a specific value of x. The problem shows both functions clearly so you can work through it step by step.
