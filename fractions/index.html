How Fraction Answers Work

When a problem needs a fraction answer, a special two-field input appears — one for the numerator and one for the denominator. Enter both and Mathpop automatically checks your answer in its lowest terms, so 6/8 and 3/4 are both accepted as correct.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to simplify my fraction answer?

Not necessarily — Mathpop automatically checks whether your answer is equivalent to the correct fraction in lowest terms. 6/8 is accepted if the answer is 3/4.
