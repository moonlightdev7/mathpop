Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to memorize formulas?

Formulas are shown in the question, so you can learn them in context. Over time you'll naturally memorize them through repetition.

How are π (pi) answers handled?

For problems involving circles, cylinders, cones, and spheres, answers are computed using the full value of π and should be entered rounded to 2 decimal places. Mathpop accepts answers within 0.01.

Is geometry practice free?

What level of geometry is covered?

Content ranges from elementary shapes (rectangles, triangles) through high school topics (3D volumes, coordinate geometry, circle sectors, and the Pythagorean theorem in non-integer contexts).
